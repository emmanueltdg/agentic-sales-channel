Technical Audit: Why "Draft Orders"?

Since the Agentic channel was recently launched, Shopify analytics is categorizing these as "Draft Orders" due to the following technical protocols:

1. Non-Standard Checkout

AI agents act as intermediaries that build carts via prompt. This API-driven assembly is treated as manual order creation rather than a human storefront journey.

2. Personalization

Bespoke bundles and custom line-items suggested by AI require the flexibility of the Draft Order backend before finalized payment.

3. Payment Attribution

Orders are drafted to hold inventory and apply the proper AI source tags (ChatGPT/Copilot) before the consumer pays via an embedded link.
